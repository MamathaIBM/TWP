Tool Guide Instructions
What is a Transition Work Pad?
It is first of its kind Transition project management tool which empowers the Transition Manager to track transition progress across Transition Schedule, Knowledge Transition, Deliverables, Infrastructure availability/ Network Setup/ Access Provisioning, Staff fulfillment, risks & issues, Service Operational Processes, Service Governance Processes for each of the sprints - completed, on-going & not initiated ones. Entire project status reporting to stakeholders can be generated based on the outputs from this tool.
The tool is capable to monitor the health of Application transition across 6 main areas at the sprint level:
* Transition Schedule - Plan V/S Actuals, Schedule Variance
* Knowledge Transition – Learning, Documentation, Assessment
* Infrastructure – Workplace, Network Connectivity, Access, Etc.
* Resources – Staffing, Onboarding Status
* Deliverables - Milestones, Contractual Delivery dates
* Risks & Issues – Risk Status, Mitigation, Impact
Business Benefits
* Provides Micro to Macro level Transition project management
* Provides deeper visibility to stakeholders with the sprint level views.
* Proactive Transition Management instead of reactive on the sprints moving into troubled state.
* Improved decision making and optimized risk.
* Financial savings from reduced rework, lost time, improved resource management and usage
* Schedule adherence & hence, achieving service readiness in time.
